About

Welcome to a space designed for rest, renewal, and reconnection.
At R&Z Massage, we offer more than just massage—we offer mindful rituals for the body and spirit.
Our treatments are rooted in tradition, guided by intuition, and delivered with presence. Whether you’re seeking
relief from tension, deeper balance, or a gentle reset, every session is tailored to your needs.
We work with pure, nourishing oils such as almond oil, lavender, rosemary, tea tree, and
orange oil—each chosen for their unique properties and natural healing benefits. Treatments may also
include blackberry exfoliating crème, facial scrubs, or honey to soften and refresh the skin.
